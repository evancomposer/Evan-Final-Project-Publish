We Are Here

We Are Here is a two-act musical about mental health in high schools. Evan composed the score, consisting of 13 songs, with Socks Whitmore. The musical will premiere on February 2nd, 2019 at the California Institute of the Arts. Here is an excerpt from the score.

Once Upon...

Once Upon is a solo electronics piece Evan composed as a class project while at CalArts. Here is a link to the recording.

Ugly Lies the Bone

In the spring of 2018, Evan did an independent study in composing incidental music for the theatre. This collection of pieces written for the play Ugly Lies the Bone came about as a result of his studies with professional composer and sound designer Leon Rothenberg (CalArts Sound Design faculty).
